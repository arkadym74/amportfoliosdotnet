Introduction


Welcome to amazing portfolios, a site that connects community of people in different development and design fields, such as but not limited to application, software, game, web, UX, UI, etc. If you are a person who is interested in learning, if you are already a professional, if you are someone who is looking for talent, please feel free, and start exploring amazing world that we are going to open for you. I am displaying my personal portfolios, and people could upload their own portfolios. In a few month to come this site will grow, and will be updated on daily basis. My website represents a portal or rather a home where people show their unparalleled talent, or where they can find their talent and show it to the world around them.

Mission


The purpose of this site is to spread awareness around development community, there are many talented individuals that require full attention. It will also connect people who want to learn, and find their own talent.
