Introduction


Welcome to amazing portfolios, a site that connects community of people in different development and design fields, such as but not limited to application, software, game, web, UX, UI, etc. If you are a person who is interested in learning, if you are already a professional, if you are someone who is looking a for talent, please feel free, and start exploring amazing world that we are going to open for you. I am displaying my personal portfolios, and people could upload their own portfolios. In a few month to come this site will grow, and will be updated on daily basis. My website represents a portal or rather a home where people show their unparalleled talent, or where they can find their talent and show it to the world around them.

Mission


The purpose of this site is to spread awareness around development community, there are many talented individuals that require full attention. It will also connect people who want to learn, and find their own talent.

Going Forward


Exploring links in the top menu will take people on adventure of a life-time. About menu has an access to references page which contains reference links I used to create my website, it also contains a page about me as an author of this website as well a link to my resume. Portfolios menu will take you to my Personal Porfolio Portal as well as to other portfolios that I have created over the period of time I have been in school as well as to my individual projects that I have worked on my own time. In correspondence menu people can find a contact form which will send a contact information to my e-mail address, and I could contact someone who is interested in who I am. Education menu will take people to education portal, where you will find training materials, such as videos and documents of different types of training in the field of technology.
